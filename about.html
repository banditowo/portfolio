About Me

I am a 24 year old woman from Stockholm, Sweden. I studied music from 2014 up until 2020 and have been making
music for fun ever since. I also started learning programming and studied at FutureGames from 2022 until 2025.

My love and appreciation for music and games lead me to pursue a career in these fields. I like to understand the
things I enjoy, and to get a look at the behind the scenes to immerse myself in the arts and express my creativity!

As a person, I like to think of myself as an open-minded, easy going and creative person. I like to form close
connections with friends and co-workers, so that we can communicate and work better together as a team!

I really want to further increase my knowledge in both game development and music production, find fun ways to
incorporate both of them, and try to be as best as I can be in both these fields (:
